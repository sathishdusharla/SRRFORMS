Our Process

The Bilona Method

Our traditional ghee-making process preserves all nutritional benefits

1

Fresh Milk Collection

We collect fresh milk daily from our own indigenous A2 cows that are raised ethically.

2

Curd Formation

The milk is allowed to set naturally into yogurt without any artificial starters.

3

Traditional Churning

We hand-churn the yogurt using wooden churners to extract white butter.

4

Slow Cooking

The butter is slowly simmered in copper vessels until it transforms into golden ghee.

5

Careful Filtering

The ghee is carefully filtered to remove milk solids, resulting in pure clarity.
